# Recurring Images

## Door

- first_use: ch001
- meaning: 众妙之门；深处通向更深处。
- variation: wooden door, sea cave, paper fold, shadowed arch, open vessel mouth.

## Map

- first_use: ch001
- meaning: names, categories, knowledge, useful incompleteness.
- variation: coastline map, star chart, city grid, body meridian chart, garden plan.

## Blank Margin

- first_use: ch001
- meaning: 无名、未显形、可用之空。
- variation: unpainted paper, empty bowl, open room, uncarved block.

## Tide Line

- first_use: ch001
- meaning: boundaries move; named edges are provisional.
- variation: shore, riverbank, breath mark, shadow line.

## Silk Manuscript

- first_use: ch001-2
- meaning: text as a living historical body, not a frozen slogan.
- variation: fragile silk, gloved hands, layered manuscripts, tree rings of transmission.

## Wheel

- first_use: ch001-3
- meaning: living skill beyond verbal formula; the shallow trace left by practice.
- variation: wheel rim, chisel mark, palm and wood grain, track fading into a road.

## Vessel

- first_use: candidate ch011
- meaning: emptiness as use.
- variation: bowl, room, wheel hub, window, doorway.

## Thread

- first_use: candidate ch002
- meaning: relation, mutual arising, contrast and harmony.
- variation: woven cloth, musical string, constellation line.
